Dynamic Pricing

Think about how airlines change their fares frequently and offer the same seat on the same flight for anywhere between $100 and $1000 - that's dynamic pricing.

The airline tries to:

  1. fill every last seat
  2. at the highest possible price.

Predictive algorithms determine prices based on customer behavior. The outcome is high prices for all the high spenders and low prices to fill up the remaining seats.

Your freemium game faces the same challenge. You need a low entry threshold for the highest possible number of players, but you also need to monetize players in an efficient way.

Gondola solves this challenge by introducing dynamic pricing for games.
